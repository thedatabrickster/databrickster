> Not everyone wants to *build* an agent. Sometimes you just want to **ask your data a
> question in plain English and get a trustworthy, governed answer.** That's Genie.

## What this topic will cover

Databricks **Genie** lets business users (and agents) query governed Unity Catalog
tables in natural language — Genie writes and runs the SQL for you and shows its work.
Planned lessons:

- **What is Genie?** Natural-language questions over governed tables; how it generates and runs SQL, and returns the query for transparency.
- **Setting up a Genie space** — choosing tables, and why good table modeling, column comments, and sample queries drive accuracy.
- **Making Genie accurate** — instructions, curated example queries, evaluating natural-language-to-SQL, and handling ambiguity.
- **Governance & trust** — Unity Catalog permissions so users only see data they're allowed to; auditing what Genie ran.
- **Genie as an agent tool** — plugging a Genie space into an agent or a Multi-agent Supervisor as the structured-data tool.
- **When to use Genie vs a custom agent vs a dashboard.**
