# Business Intelligence

:::note Coming soon
This topic is a **placeholder** — it's on the roadmap. We'll build it into a full
course, the same way as [Databricks AI](/docs/intro). Here's the plan.

> Genie answers a *question*. Business Intelligence is the broader discipline of
> turning data into **dashboards, metrics, and decisions** the whole organization
> can trust — now supercharged by AI.

## What this topic will cover

Where the [Genie](/genie) topic focuses on natural-language querying, this topic is
about the **BI layer** on the lakehouse and how AI changes it. Planned lessons:

- **BI on the lakehouse** — Databricks SQL, dashboards, and the semantic/metrics layer over governed Delta tables.
- **AI/BI dashboards** — building dashboards and embedding conversational analytics (Genie) directly in them.
- **A trustworthy metrics layer** — consistent definitions, certified datasets, and why governance makes BI believable.
- **From dashboards to decisions** — alerts, self-serve analytics, and putting insights in the flow of work.
- **AI-assisted BI** — using LLMs and AI functions to summarize trends, explain changes, and draft narratives over your metrics.
- **Governance & lineage for BI** — Unity Catalog permissions, lineage, and auditability for analytics.

[AI Functions](/docs/llm-foundations/ai-functions) to enrich and summarize BI data at
scale.
